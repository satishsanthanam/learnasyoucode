Credits: 11 rules of life

 

# Rule Explanation
1 Be Ready to Start Over Embrace the opportunity to begin anew, as it can lead to creative freedom and personal growth.
2 Do What Feels Like Play Engage in activities that feel enjoyable to you but appear as work to others, allowing for passion-driven success.
3 Overcome the Need for External Validation Focus on internal satisfaction rather than seeking approval from others, as true fulfillment comes from within.
4 Fall in Love with Reading Cultivate a habit of reading to continuously learn and grow, as it is a key to wisdom and success.
5 Own a Piece of a Business Achieve financial freedom by having equity in a business, leveraging modern tools like code and media.
6 Embrace Permissionless Leverage Utilize platforms that don't require permission to succeed, such as coding and content creation, to maximize potential.
7 Make Time for Reflection Allocate unstructured time for creativity and self-reflection to generate innovative ideas and sound judgments.
8 Understand the Basics Focus on mastering fundamental concepts rather than memorizing advanced ones, ensuring a solid foundation of knowledge.
9 Focus on One Desire at a Time Limit your desires to one at a time to achieve clarity and success, as multiple desires can lead to suffering.
10 Act on Inspiration Immediately Seize inspiration when it strikes, as it is fleeting and can lead to significant achievements if acted upon promptly.
11 Be Uniquely You Embrace your unique capabilities and desires, avoiding imitation of others, to find your true path and success.
